Insulation Maintenance and Care


Maintenance matters to ensuring long-term performance. Here are some important tips to make sure you get the longest lifespan from your insulation:

  • Conduct Regular Inspections: Look out for signs of wear or moisture, such as damage, and address them before they worsen.
  • Address Air Leaks: Seal drafty spots in your home using caulk or weatherstripping to prevent energy loss.
  • Maintain Proper Ventilation: Ensure your attic and crawl spaces are ventilated to avoid moisture buildup.
  • Protect from Pests: Rodents and insects can ruin insulation by sealing entry points.
  • Avoid Compression: Do not store heavy items on insulation to maintain thermal resistance.
